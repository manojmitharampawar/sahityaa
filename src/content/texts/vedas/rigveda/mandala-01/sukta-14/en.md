### Rigveda - Mandala 1 Sukta 14
**Rishi**: Medhatithi Kanva | **Deity**: Vishvedevas | **Metre**: Gayatri
</div>

<div class="divider"></div>

## Verse 1
O Agni, with all the gods, come hither to our service and our songs, for the drinking of the Soma; and sacrifice to them. || 1 ||

## Verse 2
The Kanvas invoke thee here for good thought and we desire riches, with those gods by whose help thou speedest. || 2 ||

## Verse 3
We call Indra and Vayu, Brihaspati, Mitra and Agni, Pushan, Bhaga, the Adityas, and the host of Maruts. || 3 ||

## Verse 4
O Ashvins, may our most honey-sweet Somas delight you; who accept our offered sacrifice. || 4 ||

## Verse 5
May the purifying Sarasvati, with all the gods, protect our sacrifice and carry our thought to the gods. || 5-9 ||

## Verse 10
Those who are worthy of sacrifice and adorable, may they drink, O Agni, with thy tongue, of this sweet Soma. || 10-12 ||
