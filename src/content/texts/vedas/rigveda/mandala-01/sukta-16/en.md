### Rigveda - Mandala 1 Sukta 16
**Rishi**: Medhatithi Kanva | **Deity**: Indra | **Metre**: Gayatri
</div>

<div class="divider"></div>

## Verse 1
May thy bay horses bring thee hither, the showerer (of blessings), for the drinking of the Soma, O Indra, who art the master of kine. || 1 ||

## Verse 2
The wise have composed this hymn for Indra for the drinking of the Soma. O wise one, open the paths for us that lead to welfare. || 2 ||

## Verse 3
We call upon thee, the powerful one, after the ancient manner, with thy might, for the winning of spoils. || 3 ||

## Verse 4
Sing ye to that Indra who is the great support of riches, the good ferrier across, the friend of the juice-presser. || 4 ||

## Verse 5
May that most bountiful and wealthy Indra give us sustenance that supports a thousand, and grant us protection. || 5 ||

## Verse 6
Indra is fierce, fierce-armed, powerful, the sustainer of human valor. O Shatakratu, conquer in battles. || 6 ||

## Verse 7
By the help of thy friendship, O Shatakratu Indra, may we conquer spoils that are a thousand-fold. || 7 ||

## Verse 8
Thee, indeed, I desire in battles; thee do we invoke, O Indra, for the winning of power and victory. || 8 ||

## Verse 9
Sing ye to that Indra who is the great support of riches, the good ferrier across, the friend of the juice-presser. || 9 ||
