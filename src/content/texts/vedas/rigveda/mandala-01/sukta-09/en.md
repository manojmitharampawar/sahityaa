### Rigveda - Mandala 1 Sukta 9
**Rishi**: Madhuchandas Vaishvamitra | **Deity**: Indra | **Metre**: Gayatri
</div>

<div class="divider"></div>

## Verse 1
O Indra, of many powers, thou art the master of wealth for our kinship. O Shakra (powerful one), give us the accumulated riches. || 1 ||

## Verse 2
O Shakra, that power and sustenance which other men do not obtain, that food bestow upon us for our service. || 2 ||

## Verse 3
We today compose for Indra a new and pure hymn of praise, by which we may enjoy great wealth. || 3 ||

## Verse 4
Who, joining the feast, powerful as a bull, with his allied army, Indra subdues all things. || 4 ||

## Verse 5
May that most bountiful and wealthy Indra give us sustenance that supports a thousand, and grant us protection. || 5 ||

## Verse 6
Indra is fierce, fierce-armed, powerful, the sustainer of human valor. O Shatakratu (hundred-powered), conquer in battles. || 6 ||

## Verse 7
By the help of thy friendship, O Shatakratu Indra, may we conquer spoils that are a thousand-fold. || 7 ||

## Verse 8
Thee, indeed, I desire in battles; thee do we invoke, O Indra, for the winning of power and victory. || 8 ||

## Verse 9
Sing ye to that Indra who is the great support of riches, the good ferrier across, the friend of the juice-presser. || 9 ||

## Verse 10
By these unswerving hymns of praise, our path is made easy. O Indra, grant us might for our victory. || 10 ||
