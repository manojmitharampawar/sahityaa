### Rigveda - Mandala 1 Sukta 1 (Agni Sukta)
**Rishi**: Madhuchandas Vaishvamitra | **Deity**: Agni | **Metre**: Gayatri
</div>

<div class="divider"></div>
I laud Agni, the chosen Priest, God, minister of sacrifice, the hotar, greatest bestower of treasures. || 1 ||

## Verse 2
Worthy is Agni to be praised by living as by ancient seers. He shall bring hitherward the Gods. || 2 ||

## Verse 3
Through Agni man obtaineth wealth, yea, plenty waxing day by day, most rich in heroes, glorious. || 3 ||

## Verse 4
Agni, the perfect sacrifice which thou encompassest about verily goeth to the Gods. || 4 ||

## Verse 5
May Agni, sapient-minded Priest, truthful, most gloriously great, the God, come hither with the Gods. || 5 ||

## Verse 6
Whatever blessing, Agni, thou wilt grant unto thy worshipper, that, Angiras, is indeed thy truth. || 6 ||

## Verse 7
Thee, Agni, day by day, O Dispeller of Night, we come to thee with prayer, bringing obeisance. || 7 ||

## Verse 8
Ruler of sacrifices, guard of Law eternal, radiant One, increasing in thine own abode. || 8 ||

## Verse 9
Be to us easy of approach, even as a father to his son: Agni, be with us for our weal. || 9 ||
