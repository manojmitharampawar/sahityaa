### Rigveda - Mandala 1 Sukta 3
**Rishi**: Madhuchandas Vaishvamitra | **Deity**: Ashvins (1-3), Indra (4-6), Vishvedevas (7-9), Sarasvati (10-12) | **Metre**: Gayatri
</div>

<div class="divider"></div>

## Verse 1
Ashvins, swift-footed Lords of Bliss, much-enjoying, accept then the offerings of the sacrificer. || 1 ||

## Verse 2
Ashvins, of many wondrous deeds, ye men of thought and holy power, come with your helpful speed to us. || 2 ||

## Verse 3
Ashvins, destroyers of the foe, come with your wide-extending car to our call, for ye are rich in power. || 3 ||

## Verse 4
Come, Indra, brilliant with thy light: these libations filtered with the fingers are longing for thee. || 4 ||

## Verse 5
Come, Indra, urged by prayer, impelled by the wise, to the house of the juice-presser, to the sacred songs. || 5 ||

## Verse 6
Come, O Indra of the Steeds, hastening hither to our sacred songs. Take pleasure in our offerings of Soma. || 6 ||

## Verse 7
O ye all-gods (Vishvedevas), protectors of men, dispensers of boons, come to the poured libation of the giver. || 7 ||

## Verse 8
May all the gods, active in deed, come quickly to our Soma draught, even as kine unto the fold. || 8 ||

## Verse 9
Ye all-gods, free from decay, masters of many powers, and harmless, accept this sacrifice of ours. || 9 ||

## Verse 10
May Sarasvati the Purifier, rich in wealth and strength, desire our sacrifice, she the treasury of holy thought. || 10 ||

## Verse 11
Inspirer of true speeches, awakener of noble thoughts, Sarasvati hath upheld the sacrifice. || 11 ||

## Verse 12
Sarasvati, the mighty flood, illumines with her banner of light, and shines o'er all the thoughts of men. || 12 ||
