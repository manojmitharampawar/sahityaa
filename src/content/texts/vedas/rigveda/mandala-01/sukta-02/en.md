### Rigveda - Mandala 1 Sukta 2
**Rishi**: Madhuchandas Vaishvamitra | **Deity**: Vayu (1-3), Indra-Vayu (4-6), Mitra-Varuna (7-9) | **Metre**: Gayatri
</div>

<div class="divider"></div>

## Verse 1
Beautiful Vayu, come, for thee these Soma drops have been prepared: Drink of them, hearken to our call. || 1 ||

## Verse 2
Knowing the days, with Soma pressed, the singers glorify thee, Vayu, with their hymns of praise. || 2 ||

## Verse 3
Vayu, thy penetrating voice goes forth unto the worshipper, far-reaching, to the Soma draught. || 3 ||

## Verse 4
Indra and Vayu, at our feast these libations are poured: Come with your reinforcements, for these drops long for you. || 4 ||

## Verse 5
Vayu and Indra, ye who know the Soma juices, rich in wealth, come ye and hitherward speed with haste. || 5 ||

## Verse 6
Vayu and Indra, come unto the place where Soma is prepared. Come quickly, with your minds intent on our holy rite. || 6 ||

## Verse 7
I call Mitra, endowed with holy strength, and Varuna, destroyer of foes, who perfect our brilliant understanding. || 7 ||

## Verse 8
Mitra and Varuna, through Law (Rita), lovers and upholders of Law, have reached their mighty high decree. || 8 ||

## Verse 9
Mitra and Varuna, seers, born with great power, widening our dwellings, grant us efficient strength. || 9 ||
