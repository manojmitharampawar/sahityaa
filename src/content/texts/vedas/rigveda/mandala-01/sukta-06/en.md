### Rigveda - Mandala 1 Sukta 6
**Rishi**: Madhuchandas Vaishvamitra | **Deity**: Indra (1-3, 5, 7, 9, 10), Maruts (4, 6, 8) | **Metre**: Gayatri
</div>

<div class="divider"></div>

## Verse 1
The people join the bright red sun, moving round the stationary (heaven): and the stars shine in the sky. || 1 ||

## Verse 2
They join his two beloved bay horses to the chariot, standing on either side, red, bold, and bearing the hero. || 2 ||

## Verse 3
Making light where there was no light, and form where there was no form, O men, thou wast born with the DAWNs. || 3 ||

## Verse 4
Then they (the Maruts) by their own power once more assumed their birth, bearing their sacred name. || 4 ||

## Verse 5
With the breakers of strongholds (Maruts), O Indra, thou didst find the kine hidden in the cave. || 5 ||

## Verse 6
As the wise sing their hymns according to their thought, so the Maruts praise the great and famous Indra. || 6 ||

## Verse 7
Thou art seen with the fearless Indra, moving together, both of equal glory and delight. || 7 ||

## Verse 8
With the faultless, shining, and beloved hosts of Indra (the Maruts), the sacrifice shines forth with power. || 8 ||

## Verse 9
Come hither, O Indra, from the bright region of the sky, or from wherever thou art. Our hymns are directed towards thee. || 9 ||

## Verse 10
We pray for wealth from this Indra, or from the sky, or from the great atmosphere. || 10 ||
