### Rigveda - Mandala 1 Sukta 17
**Rishi**: Medhatithi Kanva | **Deity**: Indra-Varuna | **Metre**: Gayatri
</div>

<div class="divider"></div>

## Verse 1
I seek the protection of the sovereign lords Indra and Varuna; may they be gracious to us at this time. || 1 ||

## Verse 2
For you two are indeed those who come for protection to the call of a sage like me, you sustainers of the people. || 2 ||

## Verse 3
Satisfy us with riches according to our desire, O Indra and Varuna; we seek you as our nearest (protectors). || 3 ||

## Verse 4
O heroes, looking for your friendship, bestow (gifts) upon the sage. || 4 ||

## Verse 5
We invoke you, O Indra and Varuna, for the sake of the people; may you be ours alone (for protection). || 5 ||

## Verse 6
O Indra and Varuna, born with many powers and dwelling in the vast, grant us irresistible strength. || 6 ||

## Verse 7
Where you two gods delight in fellowship with this thought (praise), grant us that with good progeny. || 7 ||

## Verse 8
O Indra and Varuna, drink of this juice according to your desire with our hymn; grant us much wealth. || 8 ||

## Verse 9
Sleepless, crossing over (troubles), and very kind, Indra and Varuna are our surroundings. Grant us much wealth. || 9 ||
