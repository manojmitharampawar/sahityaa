### Rigveda - Mandala 1 Sukta 15
**Rishi**: Medhatithi Kanva | **Deity**: Ritus | **Metre**: Gayatri
</div>

<div class="divider"></div>

## Verse 1
O Indra, drink the Soma along with the Ritu (season/order); let the exciting drops enter thee. || 1 ||

## Verse 2
O Maruts, drink along with the Ritu; purify the sacrifice from the Potar (priest's place). You are indeed bountiful givers. || 2 ||

## Verse 3
O Neshtar, with thy wives, drink along with the Ritu; for thou art the bestower of jewels. || 3 ||

## Verse 4
O Agni, bring the gods hither and seat them in the three places (altars). Adorn them and drink along with the Ritu. || 4 ||

## Verse 5
O Agni of the Brahman priest, drink the Soma along with the Ritu. Thy friendship, O wielder of the thunderbolt, is ours. || 5 ||

## Verse 6-12
O Indra, Ashvins, and Agni, drink along with the Ritu and accept this sacrifice offered by us. || 6-12 ||
